# Target Personas

## Primary Audience

**Ages 18-35, individual consumers, mobile-first**

This segment has the highest growth rate, views coaching as a normal investment, and is massively underserved by existing enterprise-focused platforms.

### Key Pain Points

- Can't afford traditional coaching ($150-300/session)
- Want personalized guidance, not generic self-help content
- Start motivated but lose momentum without accountability
- Want to see measurable progress

---

## Persona 1: "Career Starter Carlos"

- **Age:** 24 | **Job:** Junior Marketing Coordinator | **Income:** $45K/year
- **Situation:** First "real" job after college. Feels lost navigating office politics, unsure how to grow into a leadership role. Compares himself to peers on LinkedIn constantly.
- **Pain points:** No mentor at work, can't afford a career coach, overwhelmed by generic career advice online
- **Goals:** Get promoted within 2 years, build confidence in meetings, develop leadership skills
- **App behavior:** Uses during commute (morning) and before bed. Prefers quick 5-10 min sessions. Motivated by streak tracking and progress badges.
- **Willingness to pay:** $15-25/month (would cancel if no visible progress in 30 days)

---

## Persona 2: "Relationship-Builder Maya"

- **Age:** 29 | **Job:** UX Designer (remote) | **Income:** $72K/year
- **Situation:** In a 3-year relationship that's "fine but not great." Remote work has made her feel isolated from friends too. Wants deeper connections but doesn't know where to start.
- **Pain points:** Feels awkward bringing up relationship issues, therapy feels "too heavy" for what she needs, no trusted person to talk through things with
- **Goals:** Improve communication with her partner, rebuild her social circle, feel less lonely
- **App behavior:** Uses in the evening. Prefers reflective journaling prompts and conversation scripts. Values privacy highly.
- **Willingness to pay:** $25-35/month (values quality and privacy; will pay more for it)

---

## Persona 3: "Burnt-Out Brandon"

- **Age:** 32 | **Job:** Software Engineer | **Income:** $110K/year
- **Situation:** High salary but miserable. Works 50+ hour weeks, neglects health, relationship with partner is strained. Knows something needs to change but keeps procrastinating.
- **Pain points:** Too exhausted to research solutions, previous self-help attempts failed, feels guilty spending money on himself when "nothing is really wrong"
- **Goals:** Set work boundaries, reduce stress, reconnect with partner, find a sustainable routine
- **App behavior:** Sporadic user — needs smart reminders and low-friction interactions. Responds well to data (show him his stress trends). Might ghost the app for 2 weeks then come back.
- **Willingness to pay:** $30-40/month (money isn't the issue; time and energy are)

---

## Persona 4: "Fresh-Start Daniela"

- **Age:** 22 | **Job:** Recent graduate, part-time barista | **Income:** $28K/year
- **Situation:** Just graduated, drowning in student debt, no idea what career to pursue. Lives at home, feels behind compared to friends who already have "real jobs."
- **Pain points:** Anxious about the future, can't afford therapy or coaching, overwhelmed by choices, imposter syndrome
- **Goals:** Figure out a career direction, build financial literacy, gain confidence, move out within a year
- **App behavior:** Heavy mobile user. Watches TikTok and Instagram for advice. Prefers short video-style content and interactive exercises. Social features (community, peer groups) are important to her.
- **Willingness to pay:** $10-15/month MAX (price-sensitive; would use a free tier with ads or limited features)

---

## Persona 5: "Second-Chapter Sam"

- **Age:** 38 | **Job:** Operations Manager considering a career change | **Income:** $85K/year
- **Situation:** Stable but unfulfilled. Recently divorced, two kids (shared custody). Wants to reinvent himself but feels "too old to start over." Interested in starting a side business.
- **Pain points:** Feels stuck between responsibilities and dreams, dating again feels terrifying, needs practical step-by-step guidance not motivational fluff
- **Goals:** Explore a career pivot, build confidence for dating again, become a better co-parent, start a side project
- **App behavior:** Uses during lunch breaks and on weekends without the kids. Prefers structured plans with clear milestones. Would value connecting with others in similar situations.
- **Willingness to pay:** $25-40/month (willing to invest if he sees a clear path forward)

---

## Key Insights from Personas

1. **Price sensitivity varies widely** — Need a tiered model: Free tier (Daniela), Standard $20/mo (Carlos, Maya), Premium $35/mo (Brandon, Sam)
2. **Multi-domain is validated** — Every persona has challenges across 2-3 categories
3. **Session length matters** — Nobody wants 60-minute sessions. Quick daily check-ins (5-10 min) win
4. **Accountability is the killer feature** — Every persona struggles with follow-through
5. **Privacy vs. community** — Some personas (Maya) prioritize privacy; others (Daniela) want community. Need to support both
